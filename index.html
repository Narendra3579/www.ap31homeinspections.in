The Value of Inspection

Why Is Inspection Important?

A home is likely the biggest purchase of your life. While a property might look fresh painted, hidden defects in structure, electrical wiring, or dampness can cost lakhs in future repairs.

An inspection uncovers the "invisible" truth, protecting your investment and safety.

What Do We Inspect?

We perform a rigorous 100+ point check covering:

  • Structural Integrity (Walls, Beams, Columns)
  • Electrical Safety & Grounding
  • Plumbing, Leakage & Seepage Detection
  • Moisture Analysis & Dampness
  • Doors, Windows & Finishing Quality

How It Helps You?

Our report is your leverage. It empowers you to:

  • Negotiate a fairer property price.
  • Request specific repairs before closing the deal.
